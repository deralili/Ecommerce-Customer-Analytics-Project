# E-Commerce Customer Analytics & Data Science Project

## Overview

This project presents an end-to-end customer analytics solution using the Olist Brazilian E-Commerce dataset. The objective is to analyze customer behavior, business performance, product trends, geographic sales patterns, and customer value while applying both data analytics and machine learning techniques to generate actionable business insights.

The project follows a real-world analytics workflow, progressing from data understanding and exploratory analysis to customer segmentation, retention analysis, customer lifetime value (CLV) modeling, predictive analytics, and business recommendations.

## Business Objectives

* Understand customer purchasing behavior and spending patterns.
* Identify key revenue drivers across products, customers, and geographic regions.
* Measure customer retention and repeat purchasing behavior.
* Segment customers based on value and engagement.
* Estimate customer lifetime value.
* Develop predictive models to identify high-value and repeat customers.
* Generate data-driven recommendations to support business growth.

## Key Findings

* Revenue growth was primarily driven by increasing order volume rather than higher spending per order.
* The top 10 product categories generated over 63% of total revenue.
* The top 3 states accounted for more than 63% of marketplace revenue.
* Only 3% of customers made repeat purchases.
* The top 10% of customers generated over 41% of total revenue.
* The VIP customer segment generated more than 62% of total revenue despite representing only 25% of customers.
